What We Do

Red Candle provides technology companies with the foundation for operating computing nodes in a distributed environment. We build and manage infrastructure for projects that require high availability, security, and performance.

Our solutions are used by companies in distributed systems development, analytics, data infrastructure, and other technologies that rely on network stability and fast access to computing capacity.

Our Services

  • Operation of servers and computing nodes with guaranteed availability
  • Monitoring and management of physical and network infrastructure
  • Provision of API and data access for partners and developers
  • Support for testing and production deployments
  • Real-time security and operational oversight

Where We Operate

Our infrastructure is located in data centers in Europe and New Zealand due to network latency, and we connect partners globally. We utilize both our own servers and a hybrid operating model with individual configurations based on client needs.

Who We Work For

  • Development teams and startups building digital services
  • Companies deploying their own computing or network nodes
  • Investment groups seeking technical backing for their technology projects
